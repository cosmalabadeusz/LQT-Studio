## Audio Elements for Video Prompts

### Sound design

- Sound effects: phone ring, door creak, footsteps on wet pavement
- Ambience: city hum, ocean surf, forest insects
- Dialogue: Character says: Keep moving. (no quotes to avoid subtitles)
- Music: subtle underscore; swelling strings; synth pulse
