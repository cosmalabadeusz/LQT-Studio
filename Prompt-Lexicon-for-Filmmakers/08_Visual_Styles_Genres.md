## Visual Styles, Aesthetics, Genres

### Cinematic looks

- Photorealistic/ultra-realistic; Cinematic film look; 35mm/65mm film
- Anamorphic widescreen; Editorial/fashion; Documentary/naturalistic; Film noir

### Grading and palettes

- Teal & orange; Warm golden; Cool cyan/steel; Pastel/muted; Sepia; Monochrome/B&W
- High-contrast/punchy; Low-contrast/soft; Bleach-bypass feel

### Textural and analog

- Film grain; Halation; Bloom; Glow; Vignette; Light leaks; Gate weave (subtle)
- CRT scanlines; VHS noise (if stylistically relevant)

### Animation and art references

- Anime; Classic Disney; Pixar-like 3D; Stop-motion/Claymation; Cel-shaded
- Movements: Impressionist, Surrealist, Art Deco, Bauhaus

### Genre mood tags

- Suspenseful/tense; Peaceful/serene; Epic/grand; Romantic/intimate; Melancholic/poignant; Whimsical
