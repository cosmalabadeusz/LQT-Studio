## Synonym Bank and Prompt Templates

### Synonyms

#### Angles and sizes

- Dutch angle → canted angle, oblique angle, tilted horizon
- Bird’s-eye → top-down, overhead, map view
- Eye-level → straight-on, neutral angle
- ECU → extreme close up: detail shot; Tight shot
- EWS → panoramic shot; ultra-wide

#### Movement

- Dolly in → push-in; move closer
- Truck → lateral move; side-to-side track
- Pedestal up → vertical lift; raise camera
- Handheld → shaky cam; documentary feel
- Steadicam → stabilized floating move; smooth drift
