## Editorial and Screen Grammar

### Cutting concepts

- Hard cut; Smash cut; Jump cut; Match cut; Match on action
- Shot/reverse-shot; Eyeline match; Cut-in; Cutaway
- L-cut; J-cut; Crosscutting/Intercut; Montage (time compression)

### Continuity rules

- 180-degree rule; 30-degree rule; Axis of action
- Re-establishing shot; Insert/Detail; Reaction shot
- Split diopter (both foreground and background in focus)

### On-screen text and graphics

- Super/Superimpose; Chyron; Lower third
