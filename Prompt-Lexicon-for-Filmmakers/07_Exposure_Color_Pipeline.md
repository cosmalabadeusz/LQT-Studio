## Exposure, Color, and Image Pipeline

### Exposure triangle

- Aperture (f-stops), Shutter speed/angle, ISO
- Metering (spot/center-weighted/matrix), Histogram
- ETTR/ETTL, Bracketing, HDR

### Motion and time

- Long exposure (light trails, motion blur), High-speed (slow motion), Under/overcrank
- Time-lapse, Hyperlapse

### Color science and balance

- White balance (Kelvin), Tint
- Color temperature: warm/cool; sodium vapor; fluorescent green; LED blue; neon magenta

### Image qualities

- Sharpness/acutance, Microcontrast
- Tonal separation, Midtone contrast, Highlight roll-off, Shadow detail
- Noise/grain (analog texture), Compression artifacts
