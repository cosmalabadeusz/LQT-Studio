    05 Lighting Guide

    Lighting Guide

    Core lights

    • Key light (main/primary), Fill light (shadow control), Backlight (rim/edge/hair; separation)
    • Kicker light (side-back edge; comparable to kontra)
    • Practical (visible fixtures: lamp, TV, neon), Motivated (logical source imitation)
    • Ambient/available/natural light

    Geometry and quality

    • Direction: top light, underlight, sidelight, cross-light
    • Quality: hard (specular, sharp shadows) vs soft (diffuse, wrap)
    • Bounce light, diffusion, cookies/gobos, gels

    Cinematic modes

    • High-key (bright, low-contrast), Low-key (moody, high-contrast)
    • Chiaroscuro; Rembrandt; Split; Loop; Butterfly/Paramount
    • Silhouette/backlit; Volumetric light/god rays
    • Golden hour glow; Blue hour ambience; Neon/fluorescent/tungsten ambiance
